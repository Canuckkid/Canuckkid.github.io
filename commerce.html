US Election Interactives

In addition to contributing as a writer for The Reckoner's Breakdown of the 2016 US Presidential Election, I also created two interactive graphiics using D3.js to help readers grasp the numbers and stats we were referencing. The first was an interactive map of the United States, with each state colour-coded by the vote distribution. It also includes an info section on hover that has a graph and small blurb about the state's election. The second interactive was a scatter plot analyzing polling error. The visualization were recognized by the Toronto Star with the Award for Best Electronic Layout.

Loblaws Messenger

A Facebook Messenger Bot that allows users to search and purchase Loblaws products through Messenger. The user can also search for a recipe (Food2Fork API), and automatically add the ingredients to their cart. Winner of the Loblaws' API Prize at Hack the North 2016.
